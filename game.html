🐍 Snake AI Showdown — 7 Levels

1. Random
2. Greedy
3. Tail-Aware
4. Loop-Safe
5. Lookahead
6. Expectimax
7. MCTS

Random AI

Just picks any valid move at random.

Greedy AI

Follows A* path to the apple, ignores consequences.

Tail-Aware AI

Only goes for apple if tail remains reachable.

Loop-Safe AI

Combines tail-awareness + loop detection & auto-restart.

Lookahead AI

Depth-3 minimax planning of moves ahead.

Expectimax AI

Depth-2 expectimax factoring random apple spawns.

MCTS AI

Monte Carlo sampling: chooses move with best average survival.
